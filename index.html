Welcome

Welcome to Baba Cakes, where every slice tells a story of love and togetherness. We create custom cakes and cupcakes for all occasions — from weddings and birthdays to family celebrations — each one baked with care and a touch of home. Our passion is to bring people closer through the warmth and sweetness of our creations.

About Us

We’re two sisters who share a lifelong love for baking. Baba Cakes was born from our passion for creating treats that bring warmth, joy, and a sense of togetherness. We make custom cakes for weddings, birthdays, anniversaries, and every kind of celebration that brings people closer. Our cupcakes are crafted with care and decorated to match each client’s unique vision. As the Baba family from eMthatha in the Eastern Cape, we believe cakes are more than just desserts — they are a way to celebrate love, family, and friendship. Every cake we bake carries a piece of our heart and the warmth of home.
